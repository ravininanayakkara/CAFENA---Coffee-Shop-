about us

what makes our coffee special?

Cafena is yet another venture from the Sri Lankan entrepreneur and E-Commerce Guru MR.Perera. Cafena came into existence mainly because of his addiction to coffee, any workaholic would agree and bear witness to a condition called “Procaffeinating” the tendency to not start anything without a cup of coffee.

Cafena colombo is one of the oldest coffee houses in sri lanka. Cafena is well known for emphasizing on serving quality coffee all the time. we currently have around 7 branches and is growing rapidly.
